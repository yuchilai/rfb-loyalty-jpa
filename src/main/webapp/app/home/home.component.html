Running for Brews 5k Events

We Love 5k Running & Craft Brews Running for Brews celebrates our love for fitness and craft brews. We meet a local bars, run a 2 or 3.1 mile route, and then drink delicious craft brews at the finish. With over 10,000+ members nationwide, RFB is continuously gathering like-minded runners to socialize and have some fun. Local Beer Running Club | 5k & Craft Beers

This running club is all about meeting people new in town, drinking craft beers, and enjoying fitness with a beautiful run. Grab a friend and check out the locations across the US, Running for Brews gives local friends the opportunity to train for races, socialize with local professionals, and tour the city during a run.

Running Advice - Don't be shy, your local chapter Organizer will help you mingle with the crowd. Come with friends to the special events or join us on the weekly run. Running for Brews is a great place to feel welcome with exercise and brews. See you soon!
